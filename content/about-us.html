How we started

Once upon a time, there was us, whose journey was marked by dedication and growth. For ten years. We passionately embraced teaching, guiding countless students through their academic challenges. 

Our classroom was not just a place to learn but a space where problems found solutions and confidence blossomed.

Parallel to his teaching career, we ventured into the dynamic world of IT. Balancing these two paths, we discovered that solving technical problems and nurturing young minds shared a common thread: the art of understanding and patience. 

Our professional life in IT enriched his teaching, bringing real-world insights into the classroom, making lessons more relatable and inspiring for our students.

Our story is a testament to the power of continuous learning and adaptability. Whether addressing a complex coding issue or helping a student grasp a difficult concept, our approach remained rooted in empathy and clarity.

Our journey inspires students and professionals alike, proving that with passion and perseverance, one can excel in diverse realms while making a meaningful impact.
